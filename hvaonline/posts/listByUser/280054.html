Company Overview:
NEXTOP Co., Ltd (former POSISMO HANOI) is a Japanese company whose headquarters is located in Tokyo. We are focusing on online businesses which include online financial services, IT software development services, IT system operation and maintenance services and management consulting services. We are targeting at worldwide customers with a vision to become the most dynamic one in online services industry.
In Vietnam, we are expanding very fast at the moment and therefore, we are in short of the talented people who can work with us to materialize our said vision.
We are all aware that online companies must run at full speed to compete in every moment every day. As a matter of fact, we must run at full speed ourselves to take our chances.
We are waiting for those who can run at full speed from the heart.

Our company has a plan to build OM team up to 20 OMs and 5 OM team-leaders. You will perform following tasks:
- Monitoring and troubleshooting the financial system 24/5 for oversea partner following the checklist.
- Contacting to PIC when have trouble and response with request from customer (by chat)
- Implementing OM tasks following the weekly, monthly, yearly plan (Backup data, maintain servers …)
- Working time: 24/5, 3 shift rotation: 05:00 – 14:00, 13:00 – 22:00, and 22:00 – 06:00
Requirements:
- At least 1 year full-time experience with Linux/UNIX system administration, network administrator.
** Certifications
- Graduated from IT Network related colleges or universities or equivalent.
