Đây là BB Code em dành cho embed file media vào forum :

Replacement :
<object classid=clsid:6BF52A52-394A-11d3-B153-00C04F79FAA6 width=298 height=233 id=SHmusicBox><PARAM NAME=URL VALUE="{param}"><PARAM NAME=ShowStatusBar VALUE=-1><PARAM NAME=TransparentatStart VALUE=false><PARAM NAME=VOLUME VALUE=100><PARAM NAME=PLAYCOUNT VALUE=1><PARAM NAME=AutoStart VALUE=false><param name=BufferingTime value=15><param name=PreviewMode value=0><PARAM NAME=enableContextMenu VALUE=false><embed type=application/x-mplayer2 pluginspage=http://www.microsoft.com/windows95/downloads/contents/wurecommended/s_wufeatured/mediaplayer/default.asp src="{param}" name=SHmusicBox showcontrols=1 showdisplay=0 showstatusbar=1 autosize=1 autoplay=0 visible=1 animationatstart=0 transparentatstart=1 loop=0 width=298 height=233></embed></object> 


Bây giờ em gặp 1 vấn đề thế này.

Còn nữa, BB Code cho embed flash là thế này :

<OBJECT classid="clsid27CDB6E-AE6D-11cf-96B8-444553540000"
codebase="http://download.macromedia.com/pub/shockwave/cabs/flash/swflash.cab#version=8,0,0,0"
WIDTH="400" HEIGHT="400" id="{param}" ALIGN="">
<PARAM NAME=movie VALUE="{param}"><PARAM NAME=quality VALUE=high> <PARAM NAME=bgcolor VALUE=#ffffff><EMBED src="{param}" quality=high bgcolor=#ffffff WIDTH="400" HEIGHT="400" NAME="{param}" ALIGN=""
TYPE="application/x-shockwave-flash"PLUGINSPAGE="http://www.macromedia.com/go/getflashplayer"></EMBED>
</OBJECT>
