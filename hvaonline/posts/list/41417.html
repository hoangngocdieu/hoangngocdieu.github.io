Job Description
1. Pre-Requisite
1.1 Education & Qualifications
Bachelor degree preferably in computer science
Obtaining IT knowledge-base
Fluent English to be able to communicate directly with customers
1.1. Experience
At least 5 years of working experience of which 3 year in analyzing requirements
Basic experience in programming languages
Knowledge of testing approaches and techniques
General understanding and experience of business processes as well as an interest to understand a broad range of business models and needs

1.2 Other requirements
Excellent communication skill and interpersonal skill
Have a pro active, can do attitude
Willing to travel abroad (from 2 to 16 weeks period)
Specific business domain knowledge is a plus (banking, content management, rebate claim, health care etc)

2. Job Description
2.1 Objective
1.1.1. Main Objective: Ensure client requirements are fully understood and specified in a manner that will enable full understanding from development team. Lead, coordinate and establish requirements definition and use-case modeling by outlining the system's functionality and delimiting the system; for example, establishing what actors and use cases exist, and how they interact.
2.2 Tasks
• Gather business and system requirements from client
• Assist client in defining requirements and suggest ideas
• Create SRS (System Requirement Spec)
• Create Feature List (if needed)
• Create UC list and UC detail
• Create GBR (General Business Rules)
• Create wire frame for complex UCs (if needed)
• Assist offshore team in knowledge transfer of existing system
• Work with UI for UI design and screen mock up
• Reviewing and providing the appropriate feedback on the final implementation of the user interface
• Assist PM on project scope, estimation and planning
• Assist PM on requirement sign off process
• Assist PM in managing project change requests
• Acting as the bridge between client and offshore to clarify requirement with development team (developers, QC, UI designer) both business and system analysis view as well as the bridge among internal team
• Work with QC to verify that all requirements of the system are fulfilled by the implementation in the traceability manner among requirements, CR and implementation
• Support UAT in testing, verifying bugs/issues with client
• Review materials (SRS, UC, etc) from other BA
• Conduct BA new hired training
3. Authority Definition
3.1 Authority Reporting
Senior Business Analyst is reporting directly to Line Manager and for specific project related items to the project manager.
3.2 Authority area
Senior Business Analyst has authority on all the matters related to workflow of product and client requirement
