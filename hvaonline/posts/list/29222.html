- Tìm thử MX record của domain trên:
[conmale@home]$ dig mx vn.hff-group.com

; <<>> DiG 9.5.0a6 <<>> mx vn.hff-group.com
;; global options: printcmd
;; Got answer:
;; ->>HEADER<<- opcode: QUERY, status: NOERROR, id: 18521
;; flags: qr rd ra; QUERY: 1, ANSWER: 2, AUTHORITY: 0, ADDITIONAL: 0

;; QUESTION SECTION:
;vn.hff-group.com. IN MX

;; ANSWER SECTION:
vn.hff-group.com. 43200 IN MX 10 ms.vn.hff-group.com.
vn.hff-group.com. 43200 IN MX 10 vn.hff-group.com.

;; Query time: 866 msec
;; SERVER: 127.0.0.1#53(127.0.0.1)
;; WHEN: Sat May 9 05:56:10 2009
;; MSG SIZE rcvd: 69 


thì thấy có 2 MX record (màu đỏ).

Thử ping:
[conmale@home]$ ping ms.vn.hff-group.com
PING ms.vn.hff-group.com (203.210.157.179) 56(84) bytes of data.
64 bytes from 203.210.157.179: icmp_seq=1 ttl=232 time=292 ms 


thì thấy nó có IP là màu cam.

Thử ping cái thứ nhì:
[conmale@home]$ ping vn.hff-group.com
PING vn.hff-group.com (203.210.157.157) 56(84) bytes of data.
64 bytes from 203.210.157.157: icmp_seq=1 ttl=41 time=303 ms 


thì thấy có IP màu vàng.

Vậy, bồ có 2 MX records có 2 IP khác nhau.

Thử reverse DNS record của IP thứ nhất:
[conmale@home]$ dig -x 203.210.157.179

; <<>> DiG 9.5.0a6 <<>> -x 203.210.157.179
;; global options: printcmd
;; Got answer:
;; ->>HEADER<<- opcode: QUERY, status: NXDOMAIN, id: 2965

;; QUESTION SECTION:
;179.157.210.203.in-addr.arpa. IN PTR

;; Query time: 2 msec
;; SERVER: 127.0.0.1#53(127.0.0.1)
;; WHEN: Sat May 9 05:59:50 2009
;; MSG SIZE rcvd: 46 


hoàn toàn không có reverse DNS entry.

[conmale@home]$ dig -x 203.210.157.157

;; global options: printcmd

;157.157.210.203.in-addr.arpa. IN PTR

;; WHEN: Sat May 9 06:05:25 2009
;; MSG SIZE rcvd: 46
