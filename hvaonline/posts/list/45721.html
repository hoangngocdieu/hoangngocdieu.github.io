ping 203.162.4.190 -t (ping vào dns vnpt)
Reply from 203.162.4.191: bytes=32 time=509ms TTL=245

Reply from 8.8.8.8: bytes=32 time=332ms TTL=37

Reply from 192.168.0.1: bytes=32 time<1ms TTL=64
