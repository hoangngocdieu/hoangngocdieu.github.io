You will have a chance to:
• Join a dynamic, fast growth international organization;
• Get a Competitive compensation & benefits package;
• Receive Project bonus at average of 100 USD/month;
• Have opportunities for advanced career development and training abroad;
• Maximize skill to compete globally;

Software Engineer
Required Skills
• B.S. or higher degree in Information Technology.
• At least 2 years experience for the level of Lead Engineer
• Good programming skill with Java/.Net/C++
1. .NET: more than 2 years of experience (Core packages, ADO.NET…)
2. Java: J2ME/J2EE; JMS (HornetQ, JBM); JAXB/JAXWS/JFire; JCA; JRule (optional)
3. Database: Familiar with Oracle/Store procedure, Cassadra is optional; MySQL
• Experience in enterprise application / client-server is a plus
• User interface development experience (Java Swing, .NET WPF, WinForm, etc)
• DBMS: MS SQL, Oracle, DB2
• Self-motivated and eager to learn
• Good at logical thinking and analysis
• Good responsibility and team work
• B+ English level.
• Japanese level-3 and above is a plus.

Duties
• Work as developer in project


Mobile Software Engineer

Knowledge, Skills and Abilities
• B.S., M.S. or equivalence in Computer Science or Information Technology (IT)
• Competent in C/C++ programming
• Have basic knowledge on OOP and UML
• High motivation, fast learning capability, good attitude toward software development on mobile platforms.
• Fluent English communication is an advantage
• Good knowledge of mobile platforms (WinCE, IPhone, Symbian, J2ME) is a plus

Duties:
• Participate in activities of full software development and maintenance life-cycle in mobile application and middleware development


Java Engineer

Education and/or Experience
• B.S., M.S. or equivalent degree in Computer Science or Information Technology (IT)
• 3 years experience in software development
• >1.5 years experience with Java
• Have good working experience with UI is a plus (e.g. AWT, Swing, MFC, QT, GTK)

Duties:
• Propose and evaluate system architecture and design
• Participate in detailed design activities.
• Responsible for the development task in the project
• Participate in quality control activities (requirement review, design review, code review)
• Assist Project Manager in leading sub-group of project team for specific assigned tasks


Embedded Software Engineer

Education and/or Experience
• B.S., M.S. or equivalence in Computer Science or Information Technology (IT)
• Competent in C/C++ programming

Knowledge, Skills and Abilities
• High motivation, fast learning capability, good attitude toward software development on embedded platforms.
• Fluent English communication is an advantage.
• Good knowledge of embedded OS (such as Embedded Linux, VxWorks or RTOS) and IO ( such as SPI, I2C, USB) is a plus.
• Good knowledge of mobile platforms such as Windows Mobile, Symbian, Palm OS, BlackBerry or BREW is a PLUS.
• Experience in consumer embedded (e.g. TV, Set-top box) is a plus.

Duties:
• Participate in activities of full software development and maintenance life-cycle in embedded software development.
