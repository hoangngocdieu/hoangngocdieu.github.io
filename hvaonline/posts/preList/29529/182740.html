Here is Job Description in English

Job title: Senior Software Engineer (Telecom)

- Write software programs, including design, program, test & write technical documentation
- applications in compliance with established coding quality standard of the company
- Management of assigned internal and external developments
- Process the client’s requests, feedback and fix the bugs
- Help the clients in technical support
- To self-train on new technology and new features of useful software
- Help the Project Manager in Software Development Projects (testing, assisting)
- Make research and development (finding solution for difficult issues, studying new software, reading technical articles, searching new development environment...)
- Perform other tasks assigned by the Project Manager & Chief Technical Manager
- To attend all regular meetings related to production.
Job Requirement

• University degree in Computer Sciences, preferably telecom/networking subject
• At least 3 years of experienced working with telecom software development projects
• General understanding of telecom protocols, UTRAN System Architecture
• Have good programming skill with C++/C, XML and Corba on Linux/SUN OS
• High sense of pro-activeness and assertiveness, good verbal /written English communication, Intermediate level.
• Have knowledge of Unix and Networking (IP and ATM)
• Familiar to problem analysis and debugging tools: Purify, gdb
• Experienced working with Rose Real-Time (RT), Vxwork, ClearCase is a plus
• Understanding of Wireless Networks such as GSM, UMTS is a plus.
