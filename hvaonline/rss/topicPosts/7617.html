CISM is a management oriented certification, it has 5 CBK domains, all of it related to management areas and high level security policies that supports the mission of the organization. CISM is related to consultancy not to auditing (CISA instead is about auditing). Thus, CISM is lighter than CISSP, as you know, CISSP covers all the information security spectrum. If you plan to work in infosec management areas, CISM is the one, but in my opinion, CISSP is the right at all roles and security related jobs.
