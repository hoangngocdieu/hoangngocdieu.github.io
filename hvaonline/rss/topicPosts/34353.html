The following responsibilities are defined for this role: · Define specific roles and responsibilities of information security across the organization; · Consult with Chief of Security (CoS) on the direction in information security and obtains supports for security activities and operations; · Develop, maintain security plan and ensure implementation and tracking of ISMS plan; · Create and maintain ISMS Manual; · Conduct and maintain information assets inventory; · Review and prioritize significant information assets and security threats; · Work with Security Council on all activities identified under Security Council responsibilities; · Owner of incident management system; · Coordinate Group/Department Heads Group on all activities identified as a part of group/department responsibility; · Organize security reviews, management reviews and audits, with internal and external resources; · Gather, reviews, distributes to owner for resolutions, analyzes and reports incidents; · Follow up and monitor the implementation of corrective and preventive actions; · Effectively promote information security education, training and awareness throughout the organization; · Ensure that the implementation of information security controls is coordinated across the organization; · Ensure that security activities are executed in compliance with the defined information security policy and procedures. Working Relationships · Reporting to Chief of Security (VP level) in Vietnam; · Reporting to Director of Security at the WSS level; · Reporting to Director of Security at the regional level (Asia); · Work with Security Council members and lead this group; · Cooperate with Security Managers at other sites; · Work with all departments and group heads to ensure application of security policies throughout the organization; · Closely work with IT to ensure that IT infrastructure changes are in line with the ISMS of the organization. Professional Responsibilities · The Site Security Manager responsibilities include: · Consult with Chief of Security (CoS) on the direction in information security and obtains supports for security activities and operations; · Develop, maintain security plans and ensure implementation and tracking of ISMS plan; · Maintain ISMS documentation; · Conduct and maintain information assets inventory; · Review and prioritize significant information assets and security threats; · Work with Security Council on all activities related to security; · Manage the incident management system and takes care of incident management activities: corrections and corrective actions for all incidents reported in the system; · Coordinate with Group/Department Heads Group on all activities identified as a part of group/department responsibility; · Organize security reviews, management reviews and audits, with internal and external resources; · Follow up and monitor the implementation of corrective and preventive actions for audit findings; · Effectively promote information security education, training and awareness throughout the organization; · Ensure that the implementation of information security controls is co-ordinated across the organization; · Ensure that security activities are executed in compliance with the defined information security policy and procedures. Professional Requirements: · At least 01 year as Manager level of Security in IT industry or IT Manager in foreign companies. · In depth knowledge of security, both from a technical and a process perspective; · Good process knowledge and quality/security management system experience; · Good knowledge of ISO27001/ISO17799 security standards; · Good knowledge of risk management, risk analysis, risk assessment and risk treatment; · Good communication skills to convey security messages at all levels of the company; · Good presentation skills and ability to report to executive level; · Good English skills and ability to work in an international context requiring coordination with people in different countries and time zones. English-only working environment.
